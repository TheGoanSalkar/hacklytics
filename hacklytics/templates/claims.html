Heritage Home Insurance Policies

Policy 1 (HO-1 Basic Form) - Basic Homeowners Insurance

Coverage Summary: Provides coverage for direct physical loss to property, subject to terms and limitations.

Covered Perils Include: Fire, Lightning, Windstorm, Hail, Explosion.

Deductible: 1500

For detailed terms, conditions, and exclusions, refer to the policy document.

Policy 2 (HO-3 Special Form) - Comprehensive Homeowners Insurance

Coverage Summary: Broad coverage for home, personal belongings, and liability.

Covered Perils: Covers all perils except those explicitly excluded.

Deductible: 4000

For detailed terms, conditions, and exclusions, refer to the policy document.

Policy 3 (HO-8) - Historic Homeowners Insurance

Coverage Summary: Designed for older, historically significant homes.

Covered Perils Include: Fire, Lightning, Windstorm, Hail, Explosion.

Deductible: 7500

For detailed terms, conditions, and exclusions, refer to the policy document.
